Lassagna

Description

A typical italian recipe of pasta, meet and vegetables.

You can add some cheese and bechamel.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomatoe sauce
  • Meet
  • Vegetables
  • Cheesse

Elaboration

  1. Fill pasta with meet and vegetables
  2. Put pasta in stratta disposition
  3. Add tomatoe sauce
  4. Cover with cheese
  5. Put into oven for 15 minutes
