Taco Meat

Description

A typical mexican fast food

Ideal for a friday nigth

Ingredients

  • Lean ground beef
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic salt
  • Ground cumin
  • Tomato sauce

Elaboration

  1. Cook and stir beef in a hot skillet
  2. Season beef with onion powder, salt and cumin
  3. Pour tomato sauce over the beef
  4. Cover with cheese
